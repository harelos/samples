Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

ChatGPT for Work is OpenAI's business/enterprise GTM for ChatGPT, riding the strongest brand and fastest adoption curve in consumer/prosumer AI. Messaging leans on ubiquity, model leadership, ease of adoption; strong top-of-funnel pull.

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • Enterprise differentiation vs Microsoft 365 Copilot and Google Gemini for Workspace is thin; Copilot wins on native Office/data-gravity and OpenAI doesn't counter head-on
  • IT/security-buyer content (data handling, admin controls, compliance, SSO/SCIM) is under-developed relative to procurement/CISO needs
  • Quantified productivity/ROI proof for the business tier is scarce, so champions lack numbers to justify seat expansion
  • Mid-funnel admin enablement (onboarding, adoption playbooks, seat activation) lags top-of-funnel pull, risking activation drop-off

How I'd fix it

  • Ship verticalized, role-based use-case content (legal, finance, support, sales) with quantified before/after productivity metrics to arm champions
  • Build an IT/security-buyer track (data governance, admin controls, compliance one-pagers) to de-risk enterprise procurement vs Copilot
  • Stand up lifecycle onboarding/adoption programs that convert purchased seats into active daily users
