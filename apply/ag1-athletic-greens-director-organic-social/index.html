Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

Single hero-SKU daily nutrition powder; subscription-first (~$79-99/mo subscribe & save with welcome kit + shaker); massive podcast/affiliate/creator engine; science/quality authority positioning; global remote brand.

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • Single-SKU dependence caps LTV - retention hinges on habit formation and churn control, not catalog breadth
  • Trial/welcome-gift cohorts prone to month-2/3 churn once the free-gift incentive lapses
  • Premium $79-99/mo price needs constant perceived-value reinforcement vs cheaper greens competitors
  • Heavy reliance on external creators/affiliates inflates CAC risk and under-develops owned-channel lifecycle

How I'd fix it

  • Deepen owned lifecycle (email/SMS/WhatsApp) habit-and-education flows to reduce early subscription churn
  • Build cohort-based winback and pause-save journeys for lapsing subscribers
  • Reinforce premium value with streak/progress and outcome content to combat price-driven cancels
  • Expand LTV via add-on/adjacent SKUs and multi-subscription bundles once the daily habit is established
  • Shift more acquisition into owned/referral to de-risk creator-CAC inflation
