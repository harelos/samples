Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

Seed markets DS-01 Daily Synbiotic (plus DM-02 multivitamin, AM-02 Energy+Focus, PM-02 Sleep+Restore) as a science-first microbiome brand, leaning on proprietary ViaCap tech, 'clinically studied' language, a quiz funnel, 'Bundle + Save 25%', 1M+ 'health transformations', and an education hub.

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • Premium price + science-dense messaging creates friction for cold paid traffic; the quiz is the main lever, but sustained creative/offer testing at scale is where growth lift lives
  • Acquisition still anchors on DS-01 even as the portfolio expands, leaving cross-portfolio paid and creator amplification underbuilt - what the role owns
  • Subscription-first with limited first-order incentive vs discount-heavy competitors, so cold funnels rely heavily on the quiz
  • Clinical claims are stated more than translated into felt, benefit-led outcomes on landing pages

How I'd fix it

  • Stand up a high-velocity direct-response creative testing system on Meta (hooks around 'relief in one week', science-made-simple) to lower CAC while protecting premium positioning
  • Whitelabel creator and founder-led content as paid ads to inject third-party trust into a skeptical category
  • Use conversion copywriting on quiz-results and landing pages to translate ViaCap/clinical claims into concrete outcomes
  • Design portfolio-aware acquisition and cross-sell funnels (DS-01 to DM-02 duo) tied to growth targets
  • Bring incrementality/geo-testing rigor to paid-agency management
