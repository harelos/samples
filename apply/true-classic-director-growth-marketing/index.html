Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

True Classic sells fitted men's basics (tees expanding into activewear/outerwear) on a pain-point framework (fit/comfort/value), with heavy multi-pack bundling, an Insiders Club ($12/yr store credit), refer-a-friend and ambassador programs, flash-deal timers, free-ship thresholds, and an everyman humor-driven male voice backed by strong UGC/creator ads.

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • Acquisition leans on discount/bundle + promo timers; scaling profitably across four paid platforms needs tighter creative testing and clean measurement
  • Repurchase in men's basics is lumpy and the site is email-capture-first, but on-site replenishment and category-migration lifecycle storytelling is thin
  • Category expansion into activewear/outerwear raises CAC complexity; affiliate and owned channels need systematizing
  • Insiders Club and referral read as blanket-value plays rather than segmented, measured retention levers

How I'd fix it

  • Build a full-funnel paid system with disciplined male-humor DR + UGC creative testing and clean ROAS/CAC/LTV dashboards
  • Create replenishment and category-migration lifecycle flows (Klaviyo/SMS) to move tee buyers into higher-AOV activewear/outerwear
  • Convert the Insiders Club, referral and ambassador programs into segmented, measurable retention/LTV mechanics
  • Apply conversion copywriting to PDPs and landing pages to reduce discount dependence
  • Unify affiliate + owned-media attribution so growth is contribution-margin driven, not last-click
