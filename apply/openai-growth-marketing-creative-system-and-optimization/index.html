Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

OpenAI's growth has been overwhelmingly product-led and brand-pulled: ChatGPT's ubiquity does the top-of-funnel work with minimal paid effort, and growth creative is largely brand-led rather than a performance-tested system run at DTC velocity.

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • No high-velocity, systematic creative-testing engine (hooks, angles, formats) for growth surfaces - creative is produced, not yet optimized as a repeatable system with clean measurement
  • The free-to-paid conversion motion (Plus / Go / Team) underuses the direct-response creative and lifecycle messaging that DTC has spent a decade perfecting
  • Activation and onboarding creative is thin relative to the scale and diversity of new users; segment-specific angles (work, students, developers) are underbuilt
  • Creative production is a bottleneck at OpenAI's scale - an AI-assisted production pipeline (dogfooding their own models) could multiply the volume of tested variations

How I'd fix it

  • Stand up a systematic creative-testing system: structured hook / angle / format matrices with clean measurement (hook rate, hold rate, CTR, conversion) so winners are found fast, not guessed
  • Apply DTC direct-response playbooks to free-to-paid conversion - benefit-led, outcome-framed creative and lifecycle sequences that move free users to Plus and Team
  • Build segment-based activation creative by use case to lift first-value and early retention
  • Operationalize an AI-assisted creative-production pipeline (using OpenAI's own tools) to multiply tested variations without adding headcount - exactly the system I have already built once
