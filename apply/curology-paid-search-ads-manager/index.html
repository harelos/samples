Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

Personalized prescription-skincare positioning anchored by heavy social proof (89% report effective, 1M+ patients, 5K+ 5-star reviews) and a low-barrier trial ('first box 30 days, pay $5.45 S&H').

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • No questionnaire/consultation preview - CTA jumps straight to 'Find your Rx formula', hiding the funnel
  • Medical-consult requirement and approval likelihood buried, adding post-signup friction
  • Trial offer lacks any urgency/scarcity framing
  • Homepage is new-customer heavy; thin on existing-subscriber outcome proof and email capture above the fold

How I'd fix it

  • Match paid-search intent to a landing preview of the quiz + approval-rate proof to raise quiz-start and trial conversion
  • Add scarcity/deadline framing to the $5.45 trial in ad copy and landers
  • Build ad-to-lander message match by concern (acne, aging, dark spots) with concern-specific before/afters
  • Instrument trial-to-paid conversion and CAC:LTV by keyword to reallocate spend
  • Add email capture + nurture for consult-pending and non-approved users to recover lost paid traffic
