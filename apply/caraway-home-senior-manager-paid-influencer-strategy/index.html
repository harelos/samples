Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

DTC non-toxic ceramic-coated cookware & bakeware; design-led color-drop merchandising; bundle-forward sets; own site plus wholesale (Target); giftable, Q4-driven; fully remote team expanding into home adjacencies.

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • High-AOV considered purchase with weak natural repeat cadence - LTV depends on lifecycle-engineered cross-category expansion
  • Frequent discount/bundle promo cadence risks margin and trains deal-seeking
  • Few replenishable/consumable SKUs to drive subscription-style repeat revenue
  • DTC + wholesale attribution complexity can obscure true blended CAC/LTV

How I'd fix it

  • Build a post-purchase expansion lifecycle (cookware to bakeware to storage to linens) to convert one-time buyers into multi-category households
  • Launch registry/gifting + referral programs to exploit high giftability
  • Create a care/recipe content lifecycle to sustain engagement between infrequent purchases
  • Add win-back and warranty/registration flows to reopen purchase cycles
  • Tighten blended CAC/LTV measurement across DTC + retail
