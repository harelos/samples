Spec strategy teardown

Where you are today

Chef-crafted fresh meal-delivery marketplace; ZIP-gated acquisition, '50% off 1st week' hero, flexible 4-16 meals/wk subscription, 90M+ meals / 1M+ customers, lifestyle filtering (GLP-1, keto, vegan, athlete), Trustpilot proof.

The gaps I'd tackle first

  • Discount-led acquisition (50% off first week) with no visible margin-protecting post-purchase offer, risking discount-trained first-order churn
  • ZIP-gate is the only real lead capture - no soft email/SMS path for out-of-zone or not-ready visitors
  • Social proof is generic rather than outcome/identity-driven
  • Subscription flexibility stated but no visible pause-save, winback or reactivation messaging

How I'd fix it

  • Build cohort-based post-purchase lifecycle flows by lifestyle segment (GLP-1, athlete, family) to lift week-2+ retention
  • Add a non-ZIP email/SMS capture (waitlist + recipe lead magnet) to monetize out-of-zone and undecided traffic
  • Reframe the discount-anchored hero toward value/identity + specific chef/outcome proof to raise new-customer LTV
  • Implement pause/skip-save and winback flows tied to subscription events
  • Test a menu-personalization onboarding step as an activation lever
